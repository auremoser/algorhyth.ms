welcome to algorhth.ms

This is a host page for episodes of algorhyth.ms, my sem-web radio show ported from my existing site: aureliamoser.com/projects/radio.

description

Stereo Semantics is a semweb radio show. Each episode starts with a song, ends with its cover, and spends the intermediate playtime iterating over tracks that connect disparate nodes between the two

links

To access playlists, check out the repo associated with this page, which depending on the status of the project, will link to the mp3s directly, or port to a dropbox where you can download episodes by date.
